Pharma-Pl0x is dedicated to the development and advancement of biopharmaceuticals and genetic engineering. It is our goal, but above all it is our duty, to produce high quality and revolutionary biopharmaceuticals. We specialize in the development of drugs using green fluorescent proteins to tag certain parts of the organism, especially for use in neurology. We hope that one day our discoveries can be used to achieve a greater understanding of neurological diseases such as Alzheimer's, dementia and brain tumors. These methods will have applications in the following industries:

  • Neurology
  • Pharmaceuticals
  • Microbiology
